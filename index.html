About

Designing with structure, purpose, and precision.

I am Vikas Pardhi, a Civil Engineer and Architectural Designer from Waraseoni, Madhya Pradesh. With over 5 years of combined experience in site execution and architectural designing, I specialize in delivering precise 2D/3D layouts and managing high-rise residential projects.
My professional journey includes supervising a 14-floor residential building in Nagpur, executing premium apartments and mass housing projects, and working as an independent consultant for customized planning and modern 3D elevations.
I am proficient in industry-standard tools like AutoCAD, Revit Architecture, 3ds Max, Google SketchUp, and MS Office, enabling me to handle everything from structural supervision and waterproofing to project estimation and client consulting.
Driven by a passion for design and execution, I aim to create structures that are not only functional but also aesthetically appealing and Vaastu-compliant. My freelance portfolio showcases 7+ independent residential projects, each tailored to client needs with innovative planning and modern elevation concepts.
